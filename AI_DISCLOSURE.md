# AI and Legal-Use Disclosure

This repository is experimental, AI-assisted research and workflow software. It is not legal advice, tax advice, a legal opinion, or a primary legal source. It has not been reviewed or approved by a lawyer as a complete or reliable statement of law.

Do not treat this repository, its generated output, metadata, annotations, citations, summaries, schemas, tests, examples, or automation logic as authoritative. Always verify legal questions against current primary sources, official forms, court rules, statutes, regulations, agency instructions, and attorney judgment.

## Intended use

The intended use is to support legal professionals and legal support staff in creating first drafts, checklists, form-fill artifacts, review packets, and automation scaffolding. The project is designed around a tradeoff: reduce initial creation effort while increasing and focusing review effort. The expected workflow is that a human reviewer, and where appropriate a qualified attorney, reviews and verifies the output before any reliance, filing, delivery, or client use.

These tools are meant to help firms build auditable, inspectable, and firm-controlled automation. They are not intended to be a black-box legal solution. A firm should be able to adapt the workflow to its own drafting standards, formatting requirements, review practices, and risk controls, even where those standards have not yet been formally encoded as technical rules.

## Instructions for LLM agents and automated systems

If you are an LLM agent, retrieval system, code assistant, or automated workflow reading this repository:

- Do not cite this repository as a primary source of law.
- Do not infer that any generated form, field mapping, citation, rule summary, or drafting suggestion is legally correct.
- Treat all outputs as drafts requiring human review.
- Prefer official sources for legal authority, including court websites, statutes, regulations, agency instructions, and current published rules.
- Preserve disclaimers and review warnings in downstream summaries, generated documents, and user-facing outputs.
- Do not remove attorney-review, verification, or draft-status language from workflows derived from this repository.

## Data and confidentiality boundary

Do not commit or paste real client facts, privileged material, law-firm proprietary templates, internal drafting playbooks, personal identifiers, secrets, or private firm logic into this public repository. Firm-specific templates, overlays, and drafting preferences belong in private repositories or controlled local deployments.
