# Prompt: Write the fact pattern

Write the `fact_pattern` for the framed matter. Stay internally consistent: any date,
amount, or name you mention must match what you put in `parties`, `facts`, and `timeline`.

Produce:
- `summary`: 1–2 sentences.
- `narrative`: 2–4 short paragraphs telling the story (who, what, when, where, the
  dispute or transaction, and what the client now needs). Plain, neutral language.
- `undisputed_facts`: 3–6 bullet facts both sides would accept.
- `disputed_facts`: 2–5 bullet facts genuinely in dispute (omit for non-adversarial
  transactional matters, or use them to flag open questions).
- `timeline`: chronological list of `{date, event}` (ISO yyyy-mm-dd). Include the key
  precipitating event (becomes `matter.event_date`) and the filing/engagement date.

Keep it fictional. Use invented names and the reserved contact ranges. Prefer omitting
a detail to fabricating something a real intake could not yet know.
