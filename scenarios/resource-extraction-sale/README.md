# Scenario: resource-extraction-sale

Sale of a **natural-resource property** — a gravel pit, quarry, or working timberland with
extraction rights: reserves and royalty/severance economics, extraction permits and
**reclamation bonding**, current-use tax recapture, and the diligence correspondence. Part
of the real-estate & asset-sale closing suite.

## Models

- Practice area: real_estate · transactional (`assign_docket: false`)
- Parties: `seller`/landowner (grantor) and `buyer`/operator (grantee, the client)
- Rich `facts`: `resource_type`, parcel and active-extraction acres, `estimated_reserves`,
  price and `price_structure`, `royalty_terms`, `extraction_permit`,
  `reclamation_requirement`, `reclamation_security`, `current_use_status`,
  `environmental_status`, and `water_setbacks`.
- `communications`: diligence request → geologist's reserve verification → local
  permit-transfer / bonding letter → Phase I ESA results → closing (bond substitution &
  permit assignment).

## Key issues

Extraction permit transfer and performance standards (`38 M.R.S. § 490-Z`), reclamation and
financial assurance, current-use tax recapture (Tree Growth `36 M.R.S. §§ 571-584-A`; Open
Space `§§ 1101-1121`), reserve verification and royalty accounting, and transfer tax.
